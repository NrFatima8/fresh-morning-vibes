

# Feelfresh Café Website

A modern, luxurious café website for Feelfresh in Faisalabad, owned by Noorfatima. The design captures the essence of morning freshness, happiness, and good energy with an elegant yet cozy feel.

---

## Design System

**Color Palette:**
- Primary backgrounds: Charcoal (#1F1F1F) and Soft Beige (#F5F1E9)
- Accent colors: Fresh Green (#A3D9A5), Warm Terracotta (#E08D79), Golden Yellow (#FFD166)
- Supporting: Soft Gray and Muted Brown for text and details

**Typography:** Playfair Display for elegant headings, Poppins for clean body text

**Style:** Minimal, luxurious layout with rounded corners, soft shadows, and plenty of breathing room

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero Section** with immersive 3D scene featuring a steaming coffee cup, floating pastries, and ambient particle effects
- Tagline: *"Start Your Day with Happiness & Good Energy"*
- Call-to-action buttons: "View Menu" and "Order Online"
- Animated showcase of signature items with smooth parallax scrolling

### 2. About Us
- The Feelfresh story and founder Noorfatima's vision
- Cozy café interior imagery with fade-in scroll animations
- Mission statement highlighting the café's positive, refreshing atmosphere

### 3. Menu
- **Categories:** Beverages, Bakery & Pastries, Breakfast Sandwiches & Wraps, Healthy Options, Treats & Extras
- Interactive menu cards with hover animations (shadow depth, subtle rotation, bounce effects)
- Beautiful placeholder food imagery

### 4. Specials
- Dedicated showcase for signature items:
  - Brownie Cake
  - Pastry & Coffee Combo
  - Superfood Breakfast Bowl
- 3D interactive display with floating/rotating animations

### 5. Gallery
- Photo grid showcasing pastries, drinks, interiors, and fresh ingredients
- Smooth scroll animations and hover effects
- Image carousel/slider for featured items

### 6. Reviews
- Customer testimonials with star ratings
- Animated review cards (fade-in, slide-in on scroll)

### 7. Location & Hours
- Embedded map for the Faisalabad location
- Operating hours: *"Feelfresh is open daily—come visit us anytime!"*

### 8. Contact
- **Order form** for online orders (sends to your email)
- **Full reservation system** with calendar, time slot selection, and automatic confirmations (powered by Supabase)
- Newsletter signup form
- Social media links: Instagram & TikTok
- Phone and email contact information

---

## Interactive & 3D Elements
- Three.js-powered 3D coffee cup animation in the hero section
- Floating fruit and pastry elements with physics-based movement
- Background particle effects for ambient morning vibes
- Smooth scroll-triggered animations throughout the site

---

## Backend (Supabase)
- Reservations database table for storing bookings
- Time slot management for availability
- Confirmation system for reservation requests

---

## Summary
A stunning, engaging website that brings Feelfresh's brand to life online—combining luxurious design, impressive 3D visuals, and practical functionality for ordering and reservations.
